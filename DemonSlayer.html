Demon Slayer

Sun Breathing Black Blade

Tanjiro Kamado

Tanjiro Kamado (竈門 炭治郎, Kamado Tanjirō) is the oldest son of charcoal sellers Tanjuro and Kie. His life changed when his family was massacred by the demon Muzan while he was out selling charcoal. At the same time, his sister, Nezuko, survived but had been turned into a demon. This event becomes his drive and motivation to find a cure for her and turn her back into a human. To this end, he decided to join the Demon Slayer Corps.

Thunder Breathing Yellow Blade

Zenitsu Agatsuma

An extremely cowardly boy who was forced to join the Demon Slayer Corps at around the same time as Tanjiro to pay for his debts, Zenitsu Agatsuma (我妻 善逸, Agatsuma Zen'itsu) sometimes functions as comic relief. Zenitsu trained in the "Thunder Breathing" (雷の呼吸, Kaminari no kokyū) style by the former Thunder Hashira but was only able to learn the first form, and because of this he often belittles himself, despite the fact that he is highly skilled.

Beast Breathing Indigo Gray Blade

Inosuke Hashibira

A young boy who was raised by his mother Kotoha for a short time until she was murdered by a demon, later revealed as Doma, the Upper Rank Two. After she sacrificed herself to save his life, he was raised by wild boars, and then taught to talk by an old man. Inosuke Hashibira (嘴平 伊之助, Hashibira Inosuke) uses a self-taught style called "Beast Breathing" (獣の呼吸, Kedamono no kokyū), along with two serrated swords in battle. When he gets new swords, he purposely chips them in order to match his preference and fighting style.
